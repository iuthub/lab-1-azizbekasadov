Limitless

has no future. That all changes when an old friend

acuity.  Stoked on the untested chemical, Eddie 
rises to the top of the financial world and attracts 
the attention of a tycoon (Robert De Niro) who

side-effects and a dwindling supply threaten to 
collapse Eddie's house of cards.

As a suicide survivor, demon hunter John Constan-

comics.
